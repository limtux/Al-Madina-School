School radio is one of the flagship events of Al Madina Schools. It brings together all the actors of the school (students, parents and teaching team) for a moment of exchange, meetings and sharing. A common project that allows you to learn differently. Beyond the educational aspect, school radio plays a formative role, both personally and collectively. Indeed, students learn to express themselves with confidence in front of an audience, to overcome their shyness, to gain self-confidence and to develop oral fluency. So many qualities that will benefit them throughout their lives.

Al Madina Schools

We are a civic school, attached to its national identity and open to the world. We distinguish ourselves by an innovative educational project, inspired by international programs.
