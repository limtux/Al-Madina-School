The dress code

“The school being an educational and teaching establishment. Pupils, boys and girls, must present themselves in clean and decent attire. »
It is strictly prohibited:

• to wear pants that are dragging, tight and torn; short shorts; short skirts; longshoremen; slippers; low-rise pants (bermuda shorts are acceptable).
• to have eccentric cuts, dyed or very long and loose hair.
• to put on make-up, jewelry that is too flashy.

In addition, wearing an apron is compulsory for girls in middle school and high school (The apron must not be short and must be fully buttoned) .
Faced with non-compliance with this regulation and in the event of recidivism, sanctions (hours of glue, community service, etc.) will be assigned.

Important: Each member of the pedagogical team of the college and high school combined, can intervene with a student of the college and/or high school.


GSM cell phone use

The use of the GSM mobile phone is tolerated after the end of classes for the day, near the entrance gate only.
Any student who uses their phone before the end of class (lunch break and PE lessons included), will have their phone confiscated :

‣ 2 weeks, if it's out of class.
‣ 1 mois, if t's in class.
With signature of commitment of non-recurrence.

Important : Each member of the pedagogical team of the middle school and high school combined, can intervene with a student of the middle school and/or high school.


Delays

Students arriving late at 8 a.m (from 08:05) must request an excuse ticket to return to class. After 15 minutes late (from 08:15), the student will not be allowed to return to class and will remain in the permanent room until the next session.

- At the 3rd delay : the student will receive one hour of glue the same day after the end of the sessions.
- At the 4th delay : 1 point will be deducted from the discipline mark + a written warning.

Students who have lunch outside must be in class at 1 p.m. sharp: any delay will be penalized by the withdrawal of the badge for one week for a 1st delay, one month for a second delay and definitive for a 3rd.

Any repeat offense will result in more serious penalties.
Pupils using school buses and arriving late must claim it to administration.


Absences

Any absence must be justified (by call from the parents or in writing via email or by medical certificate in the event of illness, etc.)

Any absence not justified (by a medical certificate) within 24 hours, in a control, will be penalized by a zero. The catch-up of the control in the case of a justification, will be ensured within 48 hours which follow.
It is forbidden to leave the establishment without permission during lessons. Any student who defies this vigilance will be sanctioned. An exit permit could be issued at the request of the parents.


The Disciplinary note

It will be reduced according to the frequency of late arrivals and absences; and will also take into account the student's attendance and behavior within the establishment.
