Why choose Al Madina Schools?

Citizens, attached to their national identity and open to the world, these are the fundamental values ​​of the Al Madina Schools which are distinguished by an innovative educational project, inspired by international programs. Since 2000, the Al Madina group has been accredited to provide the IEP (Intermediate Education Program) of the OBI (International Baccalaureate Organization of Geneva). This framework program covers the age groups from 12 to 16 years old and is crowned by the APPÉSI (Attestation of Participation in the International Secondary Education Program), issued by the SÉBIQ (Society of International Baccalaureate Schools in Quebec). The educational project of Al Madina Schools is based on a quality program, integrated from kindergarten to baccalaureate, intended to train fulfilled learners, open-minded and future citizens of the world. To do this, each child benefits from a personalized follow-up allowing him to express his full potential and to fully blossom in his schooling.
