The coronations of projects are celebrated at Al Madina kindergarten

home / posts

Six months have already passed since the beginning of the year. The end of the first semester is an opportunity for students to take stock of what they have learned during this period. In kindergarten, this retrospective was scripted into a very successful thematic show. The children presented pretty paintings in the form of skits, mimes, songs and role-playing. Parents, invited to the event, enjoyed privileged moments with their children around activities of all kinds. Joy and emotion were the hallmarks of these coronations.

Al Madina Schools

We are a civic school, attached to its national identity and open to the world. We distinguish ourselves by an innovative educational project, inspired by international programs.
