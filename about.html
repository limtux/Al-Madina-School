Our mission is to promote citizenship, devotion to national identity and openness to the world. We are distinguished by an innovative educational project inspired by international programs. Our qualified team is an integral part of an ongoing development process committed to offering our learners a nurturing environment to develop their full potential and grow into open-minded citizens of the world.

strengths

The PEI of the BI of Geneva is recognized by the Sébiq and allows students to receive the appetses, an international certificate which certifies the success of the course.

International Canadian certificate issued by the School of Schools of the International Baccalaureate in Quebec (Sébiq)

Al Madina Schools offer a safe and comfortable school transport service for their students, thanks to trained drivers and assistants and regularly maintained buses.

Al Madina Transportation

Al Madina Schools offer parents a quality culinary service by organizing lunch at all sites. The menus offered are designed and validated by professionals in the sector and aim to be varied, balanced and to promote taste education.

School catering

The Al Madina group undertakes to ensure the health and hygiene of its employees by providing them with graduate nurses and a school doctor to take care of students' health.

Medical assistance

The ingenuity exhibition is an annual event that encourages college/high school students to develop their practical skills and their creative and critical thinking skills to solve design problems.

The exposure of ingenuity
